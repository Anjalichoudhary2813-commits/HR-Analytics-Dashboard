## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics dataset.  
The goal is to identify key reasons behind employee turnover and provide actionable insights to support HR decision-making.

The dashboard includes KPIs, drill-downs, demographic analysis, and filters for detailed exploration.

---

## Dataset
Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle)

File used:
- WA_Fn-UseC_-HR-Employee-Attrition.csv

The dataset contains information on employee demographics, job roles, satisfaction levels, income, and attrition status.

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Employees
- Total Attrition
- Attrition Rate (%)

These KPIs help HR quickly understand overall turnover within the company.

---

Dashboards & Visuals
The Power BI report includes:

- Attrition by Department  
- Attrition by Age
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Job Role
- Salary vs Job Satisfaction (Scatter Plot)
- Slicers for Department, Education Field, Marital Status

These visuals allow users to explore attrition patterns from multiple perspectives.

## Insights Summary
- Sales and R&D show the highest attrition rates.
- Employees with lower job satisfaction are more likely to leave.
- Younger employees show a higher attrition trend.
- Income and satisfaction levels may influence employee retention.
